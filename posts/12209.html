See Brian Behlendorf's early proposal for a State header field
(mostly to keep track of netnews-style articles read) and later
discussion of Netscape Cookies, both on www-talk archives.

There were also a lot of contemporaneous complaints about
statelessness with regards to gateways, particularly of the
screen-scraping kind interfacing with old mainframe apps.
